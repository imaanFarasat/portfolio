About Gastronorm Containers

Gastronorm (GN) containers are the industry standard for commercial kitchens worldwide. These stainless steel containers are designed to fit perfectly in standard gastronorm equipment, including bain-maries, refrigerators, and food warmers.

Our Bilge İnox containers feature:

  • Food-grade 18/10 stainless steel construction
  • Standard GN dimensions for universal compatibility
  • Stackable design for efficient storage
  • Dishwasher safe and easy to clean
  • Suitable for hot and cold food applications
  • Rust-resistant and durable for long-term use

Perfect for restaurants, cafeterias, catering services, and any professional kitchen environment. Available in sizes from 1/9 (smallest) to 1/2 (largest) to accommodate various food preparation and storage needs.

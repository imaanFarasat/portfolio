A/B Testing Template Example

1. Campaign Overview

Campaign Name: Summer Sale Promo

Objective: Increase click-through rate (CTR) for the summer sale

Target Audience: Subscribers who have purchased in the last 6 months

Test Duration: July 1st to July 7th

2. Variants

Variant A (Control):

Subject Line: "Unlock Summer Savings - Up to 50% Off!"

Preheader Text: "Hurry, the summer sale ends soon!"

Content/Design: Standard template with product images and discount details

Send Time: 10:00 AM

Variant B (Test):

Subject Line: "Summer Deals You Can't Miss!"

Preheader Text: "Exclusive offers just for you!"

Content/Design: Updated template with personalized product recommendations and a clear call-to-action

Send Time: 11:00 AM

3. Metrics to Measure

Open Rate: Percentage of recipients who opened the email

Click-Through Rate (CTR): Percentage of recipients who clicked on a link

Conversion Rate: Percentage of recipients who completed a purchase

Bounce Rate: Percentage of emails that couldn't be delivered

Unsubscribe Rate: Percentage of recipients who unsubscribed

4. Hypotheses

Hypothesis for Variant A: The standard template will maintain a decent open rate but may not maximize clicks.

Hypothesis for Variant B: Personalized content and updated design will increase the click-through rate and conversions.

5. Testing Plan

Audience Split: 50% of the target audience will receive Variant A, and 50% will receive Variant B.

Duration of Test: One week

Tools Used: Klaviyo analytics and Google Analytics

6. Results and Analysis

Summary of Results: Variant B showed a 15% increase in click-through rate compared to Variant A.

Winning Variant: Variant B

Insights and Learnings: Personalization and updated design elements significantly boost engagement, and we'll apply this approach to future campaigns.

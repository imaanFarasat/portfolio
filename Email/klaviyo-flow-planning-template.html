Klaviyo Flow Planning Example

1. Flow Overview

Flow Name: Abandoned Cart Recovery

Goal/Purpose: Recover abandoned carts and increase conversion rate

Flow Type: Abandoned Cart

Start Trigger: Added to cart but didn't complete purchase within 1 hour

Additional Filters: Cart value over $25, not a completed purchase in last 7 days

Target Segment: Active shoppers (purchased in last 90 days)

2. Flow Map (Step-by-Step)

Step Action Timing Description
1 Email 1 hour Gentle reminder with cart items
2 Delay 24 hours Wait period
3 Email After delay Urgency message with discount offer

3. Email/SMS Content Details

Email #1

Goal: Remind about cart

Subject Line: "You left something in your cart!"

Preheader: "Complete your purchase now"

CTA: "Complete Purchase"

Personalization: First name, cart items, product images

4. Segmentation & Logic

Trigger Filters: Placed Order zero times since starting this flow

Flow Filters: If person is not in segment "Recently Purchased"

Conditional Splits: Has opened last email â†’ send discount, Has not opened â†’ send reminder

5. Delays & Timing Strategy

Overall Timing Approach: Send during peak shopping hours (10 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM EST) to maximize engagement

Delay Before First Email: 1 hour

Delay Between Steps: 24 hours

8. KPI Expectations

Open Rate Target: 25%

CTR Target: 5%

Conversion Rate Target: 3%

Revenue per Recipient Goal: $15

10. Optimization Notes

What worked: First email with cart reminder performed best. Discount in second email increased conversions by 40%.

What needs improvement: Test shorter delay between emails. Consider SMS for high-value carts.

Future A/B test ideas: Test discount percentage (10% vs 15%), subject line urgency, and send time optimization.

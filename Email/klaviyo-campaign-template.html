Klaviyo Email Campaign Example

1. Campaign Overview

Campaign Name: Summer Sale Blast

Objective: Drive traffic to the summer sale and increase purchases

Target Audience: Past customers who have made a purchase in the last 90 days

Send Date and Time: July 10th, 10:00 AM

Sender Name and Email: Reza Gem Collection info@rezagemcollection.com

2. Content Details

Subject Line: "Summer Sparkle: Up to 50% Off Our Best Gems!"

Preheader Text: "Hurry, exclusive summer deals end soon!"

Main Copy:

"Hello [First Name],

Dive into summer with our stunning gemstone collection! Enjoy up to 50% off on select items. From vibrant amethysts to radiant sapphires, find the perfect piece for you.

Shop now and sparkle this season!""

Call-to-Action (CTA): "Shop the Summer Sale" (button linking to the sale page)

Images and Media: High-quality images of featured gemstones and a vibrant banner.

3. Personalization and Segmentation

Dynamic Content: Personalized greetings using the recipient's first name, and product recommendations based on past purchases.

Segmentation Criteria: Customers who purchased in the last 90 days and have shown interest in similar products.

4. Design and Layout

Template Used: Custom responsive template that highlights featured products and promotions.

Layout Details: Clean header with logo, main body with personalized content and product images, and a clear CTA button at the bottom.

5. Testing and Optimization

A/B Tests: Testing two subject lines:

Variant A: "Summer Sparkle: Up to 50% Off!"

Variant B: "Don't Miss Out: Exclusive Summer Deals!"

Performance Metrics: Open rate, click-through rate, and conversion rate.

6. Review and Approval

Content Review: Team review for grammar, design, and overall messaging.

Approval Process: Final sign-off from the marketing director.

7. Post-Campaign Analysis

Reporting: Compile data on open rates, CTR, and sales conversions.

Learnings and Next Steps: Analyze which variant performed better and apply insights to future campaigns.

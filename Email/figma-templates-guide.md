# How to Add Multiple Figma Designs to Your Portfolio

## Step 1: Find Free Figma Templates

### Best Sources for Free Figma Templates:

1. **Figma Community** (https://www.figma.com/community)
   - Search for: "landing page", "form", "dashboard", "web design"
   - Filter by "Free" and "Templates"
   - Popular categories: UI Kits, Landing Pages, Forms, Dashboards

2. **Popular Free Template Collections:**
   - UI8.net (free section)
   - Dribbble (Figma files)
   - Behance (Figma templates)
   - Figma Community (most popular)

## Step 2: Get Figma Embed Link

For each Figma design you want to embed:

1. Open the Figma file
2. Click the "Share" button (top right)
3. Click "Copy link"
4. The link format is: `https://www.figma.com/file/[FILE_ID]/[FILE_NAME]?node-id=[NODE_ID]`
5. Convert to embed format:
   - Replace `www.figma.com/file/` with `embed.figma.com/design/`
   - Add `&embed-host=share` at the end
   - Example: `https://embed.figma.com/design/[FILE_ID]/[FILE_NAME]?node-id=[NODE_ID]&embed-host=share`

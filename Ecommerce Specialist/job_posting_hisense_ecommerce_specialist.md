## About Hisense Canada

Established in 2012, Hisense Canada's product strategy is to steadily introduce a full range of electronics and appliances into the Canadian market. This includes a complete line of consumer electronics, home appliances, home comfort products.

At Hisense Canada, we take pride in our staff - they are the backbone of all that we do - and as we grow, we are looking for talented additions to our team.

For our continuously growing business, we are currently looking for a E-Commerce Specialist to be based at our Mississauga office.

## Key Responsibilities

### Product Page Setup & Optimization

· Oversee the setup and continuous optimization of product detail pages.

· Ensure alignment with global online assets while tailoring content to local campaigns, seasonal promotions and market needs.

### Advertising Operations & Performance Management

· Plan and manage advertising operations to drive traffic and conversion.

· Develop and maintain online marketing campaigns to support brand and sales objectives.

· Monitor and analyze key performance metrics to evaluate campaign effectiveness and refine strategies.

### E-commerce Sales Growth & Target Achievement

· Collaborate with Account teams to drive the e-commerce sellout and achieve sales targets of selected retail partners.

· Develop and execute strategies to maximize e-commerce sales performance.

### Sales Diagnostics & Reporting

· Conduct performance diagnostics to assess e-commerce sellout performance.

· Generate actionable reports to identify gaps, highlight opportunities, and recommend data-driven improvements.

### Budget Allocation & Management

· Allocate and manage budgets across e-commerce marketing initiatives.

· Track spending efficiency to maximize return on investment.

### Pre-Sales & Post-Sales Optimization

· Develop initiatives to strengthen customer engagement both before and after purchase.

· Implement customer review programs, competitor benchmarking, and insights-driven solutions to enhance future performance and customer satisfaction.

## Qualifications

· Bachelor's degree in Business, E-commerce, Marketing, or a related field.

· Minimum 3 years of experience in e-commerce sales/marketing or online marketing.

· Strong written, verbal communication and presentation skills.

· Solid understanding of advertising principles, campaign management, and best practices.

· Hands-on experience with product content management and online product page optimization.

· Proficient in Microsoft Office suite (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint).

· Ability to prioritize tasks, manage multiple projects, and meet deadlines.

· Experience in the consumer electronics industry is a plus.

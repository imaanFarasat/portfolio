## üí° Tips for Customization

1. **If you lack direct experience:**
   - Frame transferable skills (retail, customer service, admin work)
   - Highlight any personal projects or freelance work
   - Emphasize quick learning ability and adaptability
   - Mention any online courses or self-study

2. **Use Action Verbs:**
   - Managed, Created, Optimized, Executed, Monitored, Coordinated
   - Avoid: "Responsible for" - use active language

3. **Quantify When Possible:**
   - "Increased sales by 15%" vs "Increased sales"
   - "Managed 500+ product listings" vs "Managed product listings"
   - "Processed 50+ orders daily" vs "Processed orders"

4. **Match Keywords:**
   - Use exact terms from job posting: "Shopify", "Amazon", "inventory management", "order fulfillment"
   - This helps with ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)

5. **Keep it Relevant:**
   - Focus on ecommerce, admin, and customer service experience
   - Remove unrelated work experience or keep it brief

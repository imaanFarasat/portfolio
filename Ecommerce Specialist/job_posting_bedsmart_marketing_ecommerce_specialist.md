## About Us

Bedsmart is a Canadian furniture company specializing in solid wood bunk, loft, and single beds designed for families. We sell through Amazon, Wayfair, and our own e-commerce site, and we're growing quickly. We're looking for a creative, data-driven marketer who loves e-commerce and wants to play a key role in scaling a business.

## Role Overview

As our Marketing & E-Commerce Specialist, you'll manage and grow Bedsmart's digital presence across multiple platforms. You'll be responsible for optimizing product listings, running ad campaigns, managing website updates, and supporting the execution of marketing strategies that drive sales and brand awareness.

## Key Responsibilities

• Manage and optimize product listings on Amazon, Wayfair, and our Shopify/WooCommerce site

• Develop and execute digital marketing campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads, Amazon PPC)

• Monitor and report on sales performance, ROAS, conversion rates, and other KPIs

• Collaborate on creative assets (graphics, copy, product images, lifestyle shots)

• Support SEO and content marketing strategies to improve organic visibility

• Coordinate promotions, email campaigns, and seasonal product pushes

• Research competitors and trends to keep Bedsmart ahead in the market

## Qualifications

• 1–3 years of experience in marketing, e-commerce, or digital advertising

• Strong knowledge of Amazon Seller Central, Wayfair Partner Home, Shopify/WooCommerce (any combination is an asset)

• Familiarity with SEO, Google Analytics, Meta Ads, Amazon PPC

• Strong analytical skills and ability to translate data into action

• Excellent written and verbal communication skills

• Creative thinker with attention to detail

## Nice-to-Have

• Experience in furniture, retail, or consumer goods

• Graphic design or video editing skills

• Knowledge of inventory management and product launches

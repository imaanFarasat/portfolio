# E-Commerce & Digital Marketing Coordinator

**Company:** Pets Supplies

## About Us

We are a growing Pet Supplies company dedicated to providing high-quality products that support the health, comfort, and happiness of pets and their families. As we continue to expand our digital presence and online sales channels, we are seeking a passionate and results-driven E-Commerce & Digital Marketing Coordinator to join our team.

## Position Overview

The ideal candidate will have hands-on experience in e-commerce platforms, online sales strategies, digital advertising, and content creation—preferably within the pet supplies industry. This role will be working with stakeholders in focusing on driving online revenue growth, managing digital campaigns, optimizing product listings, and strengthening our brand presence across all digital touchpoints.

## Key Responsibilities

• Manage and optimize online stores to drive sales growth

• Work with marketing team to plan, execute, and analyze digital marketing campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads, email, social media)

• Monitor online sales performance, customer behaviour, and ROI to recommend improvements

• Coordinate product launches, promotions, and seasonal campaigns

• Oversee marketplace listings, pricing strategies, and inventory accuracy

• Collaborate with internal teams to ensure brand consistency across all digital channels

• Conduct competitor research and identify new opportunities within the pet industry

## Qualifications

• Minimum 2 years of experience in e-commerce sales and digital marketing

• Previous experience in the Pet Supplies industry strongly preferred

• Proficiency with Shopify, Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta Business Suite

• Strong understanding of SEO, SEM, email marketing tools, and content strategy

• Excellent written and visual communication skills

• Data-driven mindset with the ability to interpret metrics and optimize performance

• Ability to work independently, multitask, and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.

This is an onsite full time position.
